    | ABOUT US

    Academy of Technology (AOT), established in 2003, is a prestigious self-financed engineering college situated in Adisaptagram, Hooghly, West Bengal, India. AOT is committed to translating vision into reality by providing quality education and fostering innovation. Affiliated with the Maulana Abul Kalam Azad University of Technology, AOT offers Bachelor of Technology (B.Tech) programs at the undergraduate level, along with Master of Computer Applications (MCA) and Master of Business Administration (MBA) programs at the postgraduate level. All academic programs are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). AOT actively promotes innovation through various initiatives, and its team, Buzz 6, secured the first rank in the AICTE Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2023.
